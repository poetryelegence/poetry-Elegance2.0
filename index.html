" Main chahta hun parindy reha kiye jaye Main chahta hun tere hont se hasi nikly Main chahta hun koi mujhse bat karta rahy Main chahta hun ke andar ki khamoshi nikly Main chahta hun tere ishq main ajeeb ho kuchh Main chahta hun charagon se teergi nikly Main chahta hun tujhe mujhse ishq hojaye Main chahta hun ke sehra se jalpari nikly "

" Dekho tumhari ana ne kya kiya Kisi ne Muhabbat karni chhordi Maine izhare ishq kya kar diya Apne toh kadr hi karni chhordi "

" Yeh dukh alag hai ke usse main door ho raha hun Yeh gum juda hai woh khud mujhe door kar raha hai Tere bichharne pe likh raha hun main taza ghazly Yeh tera gum hai jo mujhko mashhoor kar raha hai "

" Jab kabhi khudko yeh samjhau ke tu mera nahi Mujh main koi cheekh uthta hai nahi aisa nahi Kab nikalta hai koi dil main utar jane ke baad Is gali ki doosri janib koi raasta nahi "

" Jab agly sal yahi waqt aa raha hoga Yeh kon janta hai kon kis jaga hoga Tu mere samne betha hai or main sochta hun Ke aty lamhon main jeena bhi ik saza hoga Bichharne waly Tujhe dekh dekh sochta hun Tu phir milega toh kitna badal chuka hoga "

" Hum akhri sans tak yar tere hain Is dil par sary ikhtyar tere hain Or sojau toh neend nahi ati Sojau toh neend nahi ati Or neend ajaye toh sary khuwab tere hain "

" Jab pukarna ho mujhe mera nam bhool jata hai Usy ishq toh ata hai par karna bhool jata hai Or usy kehdo u muskura kar nah dekhe mujhe Yeh dil pagal hai dharakna bhul jata hai "

" Ik pal bhi usy ankho se ujhal nahi dekha Main phir bhi yeh kehta hun ke mukamal nahi dekha U lagta hai sadyan hui us shaks ko dekhe Halaan ke jisy fakat maine kal nahi dekha "

" Muhabbat main Jo sun rakha tha Wesa kuchh nahi hota Ke is main banda marta hai zyada kuchh nahi hota Yeh filmo main hi sabko pyar mil jata hai akhir main Magar sach much main is dunya main aisa kuchh nahi hota Or meri gurbat ne mujhse meri dunya chheen li haafi Meri amma toh kehti thi ke paisa kuchh nahi hota "
